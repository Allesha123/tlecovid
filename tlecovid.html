Covid 19 is a virus which caused this pandemic we are going thru right now all over the world. The source of this virus is from Wuhan, China on December 31, 2019, and on January 30, 2020 the WHO declared the Covid-19 outbreak a global health emergency. This virus was spread all over the world which put all of us under quarantine and lockdowns. Positive Covid-19 patients are put into an isolation to keep them away from people and to avoid infecting them with the virus. To survive this virus you need to have a strong immune system. To avoid getting the virus you need to always wash your hands or sanitize them from time to time and always follow the social distancing ruleand avoid any social gatherings. During this pandemic we always have to be careful because we might not know is a person is infected or not.

The symptoms of the virus


Most common symptoms:
  1. Fever

  2. Dry cough

  3. Tiredness

Less common symptoms:

  1. Sore throat

  2. Diarrhea

  3. Headache

  4. Loss of taste and smell

How to prevent the spead of Covid-19:


    Always wash your hands
    Observe social distancing
    Wear facemasks and faceshield
    Avoid touching your face
    Stay at home
